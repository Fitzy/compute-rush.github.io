Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Compute Rush: Idle Clicker is a free idle game. Data processing is handled by third-party services as described below. We use Google Firebase to store voluntarily submitted feedback.

Advertising

This app displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalised advertising. This is governed by Google's privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data We Collect

When you use Google Play Games Services features (leaderboards, achievements), the following data is collected and shared with Google to provide these features:

  • Player ID – your unique Google Play Games identifier, used to associate your progress across sessions
  • Gamer profile – your public display name and avatar, used to identify you on leaderboards
  • Achievements – achievement unlock status and progress, used to track and display your in-game accomplishments
  • Leaderboard data – scores submitted to leaderboards, used to rank players competitively
  • Play activity – gameplay sessions and activity metadata, used to support Play Games features and improve the experience

This data is processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy. No account registration is required to play the core game, but signing in to Google Play Games is required to use leaderboards and achievements.

Feedback Data

When you voluntarily submit feedback through the in-app feedback form, the following data is collected and stored in Google Firebase:

  • Feedback category and message – the category you select and the text you write
  • Device information – device model and Android OS version, used to help diagnose reported issues
  • App version – the version of the app you are using
  • Player ID and display name – your Google Play Games identifier and display name, if signed in at the time of submission

This data is collected solely to improve the game based on player feedback. No feedback data is collected unless you choose to submit it.

Game progress is saved locally on your device only.

Data Retention & Deletion

Local game save data is stored on your device and can be deleted at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

Data associated with Google Play Games Services (player profile, achievements, leaderboard scores, play activity) is retained by Google in accordance with their data retention policies. You can manage or delete this data through your Google Account settings at myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy. You may also disconnect the app from Google Play Games via your Google Account, which revokes the app's access to your player data.

Feedback data submitted through the in-app feedback form is retained in Google Firebase for up to 12 months, after which it is deleted. You can request deletion of your feedback data at any time by contacting us via the Google Play Store listing.

Advertising data collected by Google AdMob is retained and managed by Google. You can manage your ad personalisation preferences at adssettings.google.com.

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data through this app, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.
